// USE CASES

Real Ways I Use Agentic AI in Everyday Life

Real examples, no marketing BS

Process Automation

I have agents that auto-sort my downloads, rename files by content, move expenses to the right folder, and remind me when something needs attention. No more "where the fuck did I save that".

System Integration

My calendar talks to my todo list, my bank CSV gets sucked into a spreadsheet that auto-categorizes everything, and my Notion pages update themselves. One agent to rule them all.

Local AI Agents

Full agents running on my laptop or home server. They read my emails, draft replies, research stuff, even write code for me — and nothing ever leaves my machine. I literally talk to them like teammates.

Data Analytics

I throw in my random CSVs (expenses, workouts, reading list) and the agent spits out weekly summaries, graphs, and "hey you spent too much on coffee again" messages. Zero manual Excel torture.

Email Automation

Not spam — smart follow-ups. My agent knows which people I actually want to stay in touch with and sends polite "just checking in" emails at the right moment. Saved me hours every week.

Training & Consulting

I'll sit with you (or hop on a call) and build exactly the agent you need. Or teach you how to run your own local setup so you're not dependent on me or any company.
